CookiePoof

Poof! Cookies vanish when you close tabs.

A Chrome extension that automatically deletes tracking cookies when you close browser tabs — keeping your browsing private without any effort.

Chrome Extension · MV3

🤔 The Problem

Every website drops tracking cookies that follow you across the internet. Popular alternatives like Cookie AutoDelete and EditThisCookie stopped working after Chrome's Manifest V2 deprecation in 2025. CookiePoof is built from scratch on Manifest V3 — it works today and will keep working.

How It Works

  1. Install — works immediately, zero configuration needed
  2. Browse normally — CookiePoof runs silently in the background
  3. Close a tab — tracking cookies from that site are instantly deleted
  4. Stay logged in — login cookies (sessions, auth tokens) are protected automatically

Built on Chrome Manifest V3. No content scripts. No remote code. Minimal permissions.
